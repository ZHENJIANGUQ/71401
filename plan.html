Welcome to Your Personalized Diet Plan

This section contains daily video guidance and tips for following a personalized diet plan to stay fit and healthy. Follow each day's plan for best results.

Private Diet Plan Day 1

Day 1: Full body strength training Training: Push-ups, squats, Russian twists, lunges, planks (12-15 reps per set). Diet: Oatmeal for breakfast, chicken breast salad for lunch, grilled salmon for dinner.

Private Diet Plan Day 2

Day 2: Core and lower limb training Training: Side plank, straight leg deadlift, single leg deadlift, supine leg raise (12 times on each side). Diet: Yogurt + berries for breakfast, beef stir-fry for lunch, chicken breast + sweet potato for dinner.

Private Diet Plan Day 4

Day 4: Rest day Relaxation: 30-minute walk or yoga. Food: Light meals, such as green juice, chicken salad, grilled fish.

Private Diet Plan Day 5

Day 5: Leg training and aerobics Training: squat jumps, dumbbell lunges, deadlifts, lunges, 10 minutes of HIIT. Diet: Oatmeal + blueberries for breakfast, chicken stir-fry for lunch, beef + potatoes for dinner.

Private Diet Plan Day 6

Day 6: Full body strength Training: dumbbell press, dumbbell squat, dumbbell curl, sit-ups (12 reps per set). Diet: fried eggs + avocado for breakfast, salmon + pasta for lunch, grilled chicken breast for dinner.

Private Diet Plan Day 7

Day 7: Recovery day Relaxation: 30 minutes of brisk walking + stretching. Diet: Fruit + yogurt for breakfast, vegetable wraps for lunch, steamed fish + brown rice for dinner.
